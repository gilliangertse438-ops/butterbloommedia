# How to Generate Leads Online Consistently

Generating leads online is one of the most important parts of growing a business.

Without leads, there are no customers.

## What is Lead Generation?

Lead generation is the process of attracting potential customers and converting them into enquiries.

This usually happens through:

- Websites
- Google search
- Social media
- Digital marketing

## Why Your Website Must Generate Leads

Your website should not just exist.

It should work like a 24/7 salesperson.

A properly optimized website can generate leads every day without manual effort.

## Conclusion

Businesses that implement proper SEO and digital marketing strategies generate more leads and grow faster.
